1. Initial state:
	1. The user has switched on their mobile phone.
	2. The user has started the app.
2. Normal flow:
	1. The user logs in.
	2. Click "find teacher" button.
	3. Select the teacher they want to meet.
3. Exceptions:
	1. No connection.
	2. Login fails.
	3. The teacher has not logged in.
	4. Navigation does not respond.
4. Other activities at the same time:
	1. The user obtains a notification from a friend.
5. The end:
	1. The app shows that the teacher is available and their location.
